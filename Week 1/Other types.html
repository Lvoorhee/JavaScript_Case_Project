Besides the normal cow milk, there are many other types of milk that people around the world drink. These could be other animal based milks, or plant based milks. Below are some of each.

Animal based milks

  • Goat milk
  • Camel milk
  • Yak milk
  • Donkey milk
  • Sheep milk
  • Buffalo milk
  • Deer milk

Plant based milks

  • Almond milk
  • Oat milk
  • Soy milk
  • Coconut milk
  • Rice milk
  • Hemp milk
